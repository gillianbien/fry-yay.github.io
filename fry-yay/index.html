How we started

Fry-yay! started before the pandemic hit. However, pandemic happened.

Fry-yay! was originally a peddling business but unfortunately, we were forced to sell our peddling cart because of the lockdown.

With our goals reestablished, last June 2020, we decided to stand up again and continue what we started. We were selling fries with flavors at first but because of the pandemic, we conceptualized new products like silog meals and burgers.
